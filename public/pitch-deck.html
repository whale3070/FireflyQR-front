Problem / 问题

In cross-border distribution, sales and usage data is often opaque, delayed, or manipulated. Brands and publishers cannot reliably verify where, when, and how products were actually sold or redeemed.

在跨境分销中，销量与使用数据往往不透明、延迟或可被篡改。 品牌与出版社无法可靠验证产品在哪里、什么时候、以及如何被实际售出或兑换。

  • ❌Channel reports are not trustworthy (no audit trail) / 渠道报告不可验证（缺少审计追踪）
  • ❌Reconciliation is slow and manual across platforms/countries / 跨平台、跨国家对账慢且依赖人工
  • ❌Payments and sales data are disconnected / 支付与销售数据割裂，难以形成闭环
Solution (one-line) / 解决方案（一句话）

Real‑world Action → On‑chain Proof → Automated Settlement

真实行为 → 链上凭证 → 自动结算

Each scan/redeem produces a verifiable on-chain event on Conflux, making sales data queryable and enabling stablecoin settlement.

每一次扫码/兑换都会在 Conflux 上生成可验证的链上事件，让销量数据可查询、可审计，并支持稳定币结算与对账自动化。

How It Works / 工作原理

Each product (physical or digital) is bound to an on‑chain contract. When a customer scans a QR code or redeems a code, a real sales event is generated.

每个实体或数字商品都会绑定到链上合约；当用户扫码/兑换时，会生成一次真实销售事件。

Pipeline / 流程
  • 1📦 Real‑world scan/redeem / 现实世界扫码或兑换
  • 2🔗 Event recorded on Conflux eSpace (timestamped, verifiable) / Conflux eSpace 记录事件（可验证、带时间戳）
  • 3💰 Stablecoin settlement (USDT) / 稳定币结算（USDT）
  • 4📊 Queryable data for reconciliation & analytics / 可查询数据用于对账与分析
Web2-friendly UX / Web2 友好体验

Users do not need wallets or native tokens. Gas is abstracted via relayers, and can be upgraded with EIP‑7702 account abstraction.

用户无需安装钱包或持有原生币；通过 relayer 进行 gas 代付，并可升级到 EIP‑7702 账户抽象方案。
